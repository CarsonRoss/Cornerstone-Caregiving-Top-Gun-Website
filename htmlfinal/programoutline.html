In just five-weeks, emerge as an Operating Director, an essential role in spearheading the nationwide growth of Cornerstone Caregiving.

Week 1:

CORPORATE TRAINING

- Evolutionized corporate training led by company founders in Waco, TX

- Network with existing Operating Directors

- Connect with established corporate leaders

Weeks 2-5:

FIELD TRAINING

- Shadow experienced Operating Director in an existing office

- Learn roles and take on responsibilities of the day-to-day operations

- Incrementally take on the marketing, recruiting, and scheduling tasks of an Operating Director

- Deep dive into P&L and Budget Management statements

- Establish and maintain relationships with clients throughout the community

FINAL PRESENTATION

- Return to Waco, TX to present to founders and corporate leaders

- Display greatest achievements and challenges throughout the program

- Successfully passing all phases guarantees you an Operating Director position

This paid program for early professionals provides hands-on experience in managing and expanding a company branch. Receive thorough training, followed by a month immersed in branch operations with weekly evaluations.



After shadowing one of our existing directors and incrementally assuming their responsibilities, walk away as a full time Operating Director. Successful participants are relocated to launch and grow their own office around the United States.


As a Top Gun Participant, you will gain practical insight into marketing, operations, finances, and management. Key responsibilities include building relationships with referral sources, the scheduling and recruiting of caregivers.
