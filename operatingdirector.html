Become an Operating Director

After completion of the Top Gun Program and proceeding through the hiring process, our Operating Directors play a pivotal role in optimizing the growth and development of Cornerstone through a multi-faceted approach. You will manage and actively expand your office, relishing in uncapped growth, a lucrative compensation package, and watching your business take off.

- Ongoing training and professional development

Roles and Responsibilites

As the director, you will independently manage and lead a branch of Cornerstone Caregiving, along with unparalleled and ongoing corporate training and support.

Business Development:

Develop and execute a marketing plan to establish Cornerstone as the preferred in-home care provider.

Referral Building:

Cultivate and manage relationships with referral partners (hospitals, hospices, senior living, etc.).

Staffing and Scheduling:

Hiring and onboarding new team members and ensuring all shifts are property staffed. Monitor on-call responsibilities as calls come from clients, caregiving and partners.

In-Home Assessments:

Build strong client relationships, conduct intakes, and ensure client satisfaction.

Financial Management:

Oversee office budget that is reflected in profit and loss statements.

Cultivating Culture:

Creating the workplace of choice for your territory. Set your own standard through incentivizing, motivating, and setting the tone of your team morale.
