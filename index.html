Program Highlights

  • 1. Compensation
      - All expenses for travel, meals, and lodging are covered for the entire 5-week program
      - In addition to all travel being covered, candidates receive a weekly stipend of $1000
  • 2. Competition
      - This program fosters competition with a performace-based scoring system
      - The ability to compete against the top young professionals in the U.S.
      - Candidates are graded from day one until completion
  • 3. Training
      - Kick-off the first week with founder-led corporate training in Waco, TX
      - The following 4 weeks include working under a current Operating Director with a proven history of success in one of our nationwide offices
      - Gain hands on experience in high-level decision-making, including hiring and firing
      - Take ownership of a P&L statement, learning essential financial management skills
  • 4. Mentorship
      - Participants benefit from one-on-one mentorship with experienced directors
      - Gain valuable insights and consistent feedback throughout the program
  • 5. Career Growth
      - A chance to walk away with all the knowledge and tools you need to lead, develop, and grow a company

Why Top Gun

At Cornerstone Caregiving, we believe in unlocking potential, especially in a rising generation of leaders. Our mission is clear: provide elite young professionals a chance to showcase their abilites, and give them a chance to become a pillar of our company, an Operating Director.

The Top Gun Program actively shapes emerging leaders. Understanding the true growth comes from hand-on experience, this program prepares our canidates to thrive. On top of that, canidates are challenged to overcome obstacles and develop leadership skills. Our canidates are entrusted with their own office where they gain understanding by experience.

Our commitment transcends conventional mentorship; we create a dynamic learning environment where candiates not only learn but actively contribute to shaping the future of our business. Embark on a journey where untapped potential converges with unparalleled opportunity, ultimately shaping ones future as a business leader.
